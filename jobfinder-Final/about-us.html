About Us

OUR OBJECTIVE

The main objective of this project is to build a website for a Employment business, through which the job seekers can find there perfect companies to work for and job providers can find workers i.e. job seekers.

  • The aim of the proposed system is to design a system using which one can perform all operations related to Employment.
  • The user registers their details and is allowed to choose between Job Provider or Job Seeker
  • Job Provider can add categories and subcategories for faster and efficient filtering of potential freelancers/employees
  • Job seeker can filter jobs on the basis of categories and subcategories they want to apply for.
COMPANY PROFILE
The company is now planning to enter the IT sector and plans to launch the “Employment Oriented Portal” in order to find skilled potential employees and to make their name not only as a firm which deals in Threads but also as an IT company.
